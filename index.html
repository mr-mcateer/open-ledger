How the System Operates

The operational logic of dynastic power, drawn from documented patterns and investigative findings.

01

Institutional Credentialing

Private wealth funds foundations (Rockefeller, Ford, etc.) that produce the personnel who staff government agencies. The institutions are the pipeline.

02

Financial Insurance via Compromat

If intelligence or offshore money is invested through intermediaries, the person whose name is on the account must be compromised — otherwise they could steal the money. Compromat is financial insurance, not just blackmail.

03

Regulatory Capture

Personnel rotate between private firms and the government agencies supposed to police them. Jay Clayton's career path — Sullivan & Cromwell → SEC → Apollo → SDNY — is the template.

04

Bipartisan Coverage

Key players like Leon Black donate to all politicians on both sides. The system doesn't depend on which party holds power — it owns the infrastructure.

05

Opacity by Design

Private companies (Koch Industries, Cargill, Mars), opaque holding structures (Rothschild trusts), and offshore vehicles ensure wealth remains unquantifiable.

06

The Domino Theory

Insiders believe: if one person is prosecuted without hope of pardon, many others will cooperate because they must believe they'll go to prison if they don't. The system's stability depends on no one breaking.

"They didn't build a conspiracy. They built an operating system — one where private wealth funds the institutions that produce policy, intelligence operations protect the money flows, compromat ensures loyalty, and the legal/regulatory apparatus is staffed by people who rotate between private firms and government."
